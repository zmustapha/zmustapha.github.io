A mapping application for spatial and chronological thinking

Visualize. Analyze. Collaborate.

Learning with time and space

Locus Tempus is a powerful but simple application designed to easily facilitate map-based learning activities across disciplines. Locus Tempus features tools for authoring maps, inviting collaborators, and analyzing data spatially and chronologically.

Visualize

Foster spatial reasoning by using map layers to visualize geospatial data from different sources over time.

Analyze

Tag, connect, and categorize events and layers to show patterns, trends, and relationships in your data for chronological and spatial reasoning.

Collaborate

Share your map and allow others to contribute to your visualization and analysis. For higher education, Locus Tempus may be integrated into any content management system providing faculty a streamlined process for authoring maps and inviting student contributors.
